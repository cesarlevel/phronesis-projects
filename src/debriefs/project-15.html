What did you find confusing?

The documentation guided us on setting up the test just fine. The branching on 3DS was not very clear initially, as we used the filters on the gateway form page (bankCountry in CA) and no challange was happening on the deposit form. We did check on the transaction details page that the filter was working fine (even if we didn't know how exactly it worked under the hood which was a surprise as there are many option for countries in the filters) and the 3DS checked happened in a frictionless way. Took us a bit until we noticed in the documation that the only card that works to show the challange was an US one (BIN 41111).
